Quick Noodle-Veggie Bowls

This simple noodle bowl recipe is super adaptable. Feel free to swap out the mushrooms and snow peas for other quick-cooking veggies, such as broccoli, cabbage, or green beans.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • ¾ cup shredded carrot
  • 4 medium (4-1/8" long)s green onions, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 8 ounces cooked spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add mushrooms, snow peas, carrot, and green onions.
  3. Cook, stirring, until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes.
  4. Add garlic; cook 1 minute more.
  5. Stir together soy sauce, sugar, and cayenne in a small bowl; pour into skillet.
  6. Add cooked spaghetti and toss to coat.
  7. Cook until heated through, about 1 minute.
  8. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds.
